Apps & Desktops

It used to be that a business had to invest an incredible amount in a company-wide computer infrastructure in order to be viable. These huge systems not only come with immense costs, but also invoke ongoing upkeep and non-dedicated support that could leave you in the lurch should any technical issues arise.

Instead of being held back by such static systems, Deskserve offers a variety of Tariff subscription options, giving you a wide selection of cloud and app-access services that fit and flex to your needs. You can pick-and-choose exactly what features you desire, add-on extra apps, and customise database back-up and storage features. Then you can sit back and enjoy having an expert IT team supporting your company growth and connectivity at all times!

Better yet, if you start using a service or app and realise it’s not as necessary as you thought, you can drop it right away so it doesn’t cost you down the road. This is cloud-empowerment made easy.
